Dog Care Tips

Dogs are loyal and affectionate pets that require proper care to stay healthy and happy. Here are some essential tips:

  • Diet: Provide a balanced diet with high-quality dog food and avoid feeding human food.
  • Exercise: Daily walks and playtime are crucial for physical and mental health.
  • Grooming: Regular grooming helps keep your dog clean and comfortable.
  • Veterinary Care: Schedule annual check-ups and keep up with vaccinations.
