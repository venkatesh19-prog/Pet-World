Cat Care Tips

Cats are independent yet loving pets. Here are some tips to keep your cat content and healthy:

  • Diet: Feed high-protein cat food and ensure fresh water is always available.
  • Litter Box: Clean the litter box daily and place it in a quiet location.
  • Grooming: Brush your cat regularly to reduce shedding.
  • Playtime: Engage your cat with toys for physical and mental stimulation.
